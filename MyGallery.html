1.Illustrations

Since childhood, my eyes always would bright shine to colors, shapes and lines. Since my very early childhood, I started sketching and drawing. The more I grew up and became mature the more interested I became in the matter and the faster I realized I coudl be good at it. I was only during the end of high school though and through my preparation for college that I started investing more time in drawing and paiting, wiht simple and cheap materials. After I moved to the united stated in 2019 I invested more time and dedication to learn digital ilustration, which is something I am much more confortable doing now. Nowadays, I ilustrate in both traditional and digital formats with different softwares selections and materilas as well.

1.1 Traditional

For my traditional style, I mostly use watercolor, gouache, markers and colorpencils to finalize my ilustrations. I like to have at least 3 different selections of pen sizes in oder to draw my artline.

Digital ilustration is one of my biggest pations at the moment, and certainly one I want to learn more and more everyday. It something that not only relaxes me but also makes me feel good about myself. I mostly ilustrate in procreate and now I am starting to explore ilustrator
